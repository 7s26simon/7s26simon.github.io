Research and Writeups

I purchased a medium subscription expecting to create a lot of content on here. I did achieve that, but not the way I wanted to. You see, unfortunately, even with a premium medium account, you’re still limited to 2 posts in 24 hours. This is…annoying. As a paying member, you shouldn’t be limited. And that’s why I’m making a decision to move my writeups back to github.

My medium account wont be going anywhere, as I will post anything I see fit, whether it’s research, news, hot-takes or whatever i’m eating that day, with a selfie or two - that stuff will go here. For me, it makes sense to separate the two. That way, medium will give me some freedom to post just about anything I want, not just write-ups.

So, if you’d like to continue following me in any capacity, you can remain subscribed (or bookmark this page), or you’ll find any new write-ups over on https://7s26simon.github.io/ (I am in the process of revamping the pages to make it fresher and modern than it was).

Thank you for your support!
